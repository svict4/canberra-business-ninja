The factors

Education

Education uses the distances to schools, universities and TAFEs.

Safety

Safety takes into account police stations and crime rates.

Nature

Nature includes parks, BBQ areas, pet areas, etc

Health

Health takes into account data about hospitals, fitness grounds, etc

Housing Affordability

Takes into account weekly rental averages per suburb

Transport

Accessibility to public transport and taking into account high cyclist accidents
